Taylor Annart

Textile Artist



Taylor Annart is an artist who uses bridal wear as her canvas and believes that nothing is more beautiful than showing your true colours. She creates each of her works so that you can feel like a bride without giving up on feeling like yourself.

Making art for a living was a lifelong goal of Taylors and after years of attempts and failures, getting engaged was what became the moment that launched her art career. So far unable to make it as an artist full-time, Taylor switched her efforts to pouring out her soul and creativity into her wedding day. As someone who wears their heart on their sleeve, and sees the world with 100x saturation, Taylor added colour to her hair, her dress, and speckled it all over the venue with handmade flowers and centerpieces. Bridal but colourful, traditional yet unique, bold but elegant, the dyed wedding dress made a perfect fit for her.

Post wedding and low on savings, Taylor was running out of hope for her art career. She considered selling her wedding dress, but then thought who would buy a painted dress? It was September 4th, 2016, when suddenly a UK gossip blog posted a picture of Taylor in her painted dress and skyrocketed into viral wedding madness. Some loved it, some hated it, and hundreds searched for Taylor to become clients. All she could say was yes.

Since then, Taylors life has taken quite a few twists and turns, but her business Canvas Bridal has stayed and evolved with her. From married to divorced, from city to city, and from custom pieces to up-cycling one of a kind gowns.
